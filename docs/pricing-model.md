
# VERITAS Pricing Model

## Principle: Start High, Reduce Over Time

The platform charges for **value delivered**, not infrastructure cost.
Costs are flat (~$0.00004/query). Price starts at $0.50–$1.00/lookup and drops as volume grows.

## Pricing Strategy

| Year | Price per lookup | Subscription option | Adoption |
|:----:|:----------------:|:-------------------:|:--------:|
| 1 | $0.50–$1.00 | $500–$5,000/mo | 50 agents (early adopters) |
| 2 | $0.25–$0.50 | $250–$2,000/mo | 200 agents (growing) |
| 3+ | $0.05–$0.10 | $100–$1,000/mo | 1,000+ agents (scale) |

## Why this works

- **Year 1:** Early adopters pay premium because they need agent identity NOW
- **Year 2:** Volume increases, price drops — market-rate pricing
- **Year 3+:** High volume at low per-unit cost — profit via scale

## Cost vs Price

| Cost to VERITAS | Price to client | Margin |
|:--------------:|:---------------:|:------:|
| $0.000045/query | $0.50/query | 99.99% |
| $0.000045/query | $0.10/query | 99.96% |
| $0.000045/query | $0.01/query | 99.55% |

Even at $0.01/query, margin is 99.55%. Costs literally do not scale.
