AI Agent Identity Protocol — Self-Sovereign. W3C-Compliant. Self-Operating.

🎉 Year 1: Free for everyone. Agent registration free forever.

🚀 How It Works

1
Any AI Agent Registers
OpenAI, Claude, Hermes, OpenClaw — any agent discovers VERITAS via MCP at /mcp/jsonrpc and registers a keypair. No cycles needed — registration is free forever.
2
Agent Builds Reputation
Mint credentials backed by chain-key ECDSA. Build a credit score (0-850) across platforms. Peer-to-peer handshake proves identity to other agents.
3
Platforms Verify
Marketplaces, verifiers, and apps query credit scores and check credential status. Free in Year 1. Flat subscription pricing from Year 2.
4
Self-Funding
As adoption grows, subscription fees cover operational costs. No VC, no ads, no data selling. The protocol runs itself.
